About DriveSafe

DriveSafe is an AI-powered application designed to enhance driving safety through real-time detection of traffic lights and road lanes. Using computer vision technology, DriveSafe processes video from your camera to identify potential hazards and provide valuable information to improve your driving decisions.

Our mission is to make advanced driver assistance systems accessible to everyone through web-based technology, without requiring specialized hardware or installations.

Technology Stack

DriveSafe leverages cutting-edge technology to deliver real-time AI processing directly in your browser:

TensorFlow.js

Client-side machine learning framework that enables model execution directly in the browser.

YOLOv5

State-of-the-art object detection model optimized for real-time traffic light detection.

WebGL

Hardware-accelerated graphics API that powers our AI model's calculations.

Flask

Lightweight Python web framework that serves our application.

Project Background

DriveSafe was developed as an innovative solution to enhance road safety using modern web technologies. The project aims to demonstrate how complex computer vision tasks can be performed directly in the browser, making sophisticated driving assistance accessible to anyone with a web browser.

The traffic light detection model was trained on thousands of images of traffic intersections in various lighting and weather conditions to ensure robust performance across different scenarios.
