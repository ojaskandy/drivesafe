How It Works

DriveSafe uses advanced machine learning models running directly in your browser. Our models are trained on thousands of traffic scenarios to accurately detect traffic lights and road lanes in various lighting and weather conditions.

The application is built using client-side technology, ensuring your data stays on your device for maximum privacy and performance.
